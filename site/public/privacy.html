🎮 Privacy Policy

Cube Match 3D · Last updated: June 12, 2026

Cube Match 3D ("the App") is developed by NextAd ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require an account and does not ask for personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

Usage analytics. We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the App is used so we can improve it. This includes anonymous, aggregated information such as:

  • Gameplay events (for example, levels started and completed, features used)
  • App usage (for example, session counts and screens viewed)
  • General device information (device model, operating system version, language, and country/region)
  • A randomly generated app-instance identifier

This information is not used to identify you personally.

On-device data. Your game progress and settings are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to us.

2. How We Use Information

We use analytics information to operate and improve the App, understand which features are used, measure engagement and retention, and diagnose problems.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Firebase / Google Analytics to collect and process the analytics information described above. Google processes this data in accordance with its own policies. See Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your information, and we do not share personal information with third parties. Analytics data is processed by Google acting as our service provider.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

6. Your Choices

You can limit analytics collection through your device's privacy settings, and you can remove all locally stored game data by uninstalling the App.

7. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained by Google in accordance with Firebase Analytics' data-retention settings. Data stored on your device remains until you clear it or uninstall the App.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. International Users

Analytics information may be processed on servers operated by Google in the United States and other countries.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

11. Account Deletion

If you signed in with Google and wish to delete your account and associated data, please send an email to dev@nextad.ai with the subject line "Delete My Account - Cube Match 3D". We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Data that will be deleted: your Google account link and game progress stored on our servers.
Data that will be retained: anonymized analytics data that cannot be linked back to you.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at dev@nextad.ai.
